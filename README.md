# NaijaPayCalc

> Stop undercharging. Know exactly what to charge, what gets deducted, and what lands in your account.

## What it does

NaijaPayCalc helps Nigerian freelancers calculate:
- The right rate to charge clients (USD)
- Live conversion to Naira using real exchange rates
- Exact deductions for Raenest, Payoneer, Wise and Upwork
- Estimated VAT
- What you actually take home

## Who it's for

Any Nigerian freelancer — developer, designer, writer, PM — who has ever wondered "am I charging enough?" or "how much will I actually receive?"

## Why it matters

Nigerian freelancers consistently undercharge international clients by 30–50%. This tool gives you the data to negotiate confidently and stop leaving money on the table.

## Tech
- Vanilla HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Live exchange rate from open.er-api.com
- Zero dependencies, works in any browser
